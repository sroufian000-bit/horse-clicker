# 🐴 Horse Clicker

A fun and addictive horse clicker game where you click to earn points and spend them on upgrades to increase your earning potential!

## Features

- **Click to Earn**: Click the horse to earn points
- **6 Unique Upgrades**: Each upgrade increases your points per click by a different multiplier:
  - 🔨 Horseshoes (+1.5x multiplier, 10 points)
  - 🎀 Saddle (+2x multiplier, 50 points)
  - 🥕 Carrot (+3x multiplier, 100 points)
  - ⚡ Lightning Strike (+5x multiplier, 250 points)
  - 🏆 Trophy (+10x multiplier, 500 points)
  - 👑 Crown (+15x multiplier, 1000 points)

- **Persistent Game State**: Your progress is automatically saved to browser storage
- **Statistics Tracking**: View your total clicks, points per click, and total points earned
- **Responsive Design**: Play on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices

## How to Play

1. **Click the Horse**: Click the horse emoji to earn points
2. **Buy Upgrades**: Use your points to buy upgrades from the upgrades panel
3. **Increase Multipliers**: Each upgrade you own increases your points per click
4. **Scale Up**: Purchase multiple copies of the same upgrade for even greater multipliers
5. **Progress**: Work your way up to unlock and purchase the rare Crown upgrade!

## Game Mechanics

- Base points per click: 1
- Each upgrade adds its multiplier value per copy owned
- Upgrades are purchased with in-game points
- Progress is automatically saved every 5 seconds
- You can own multiple copies of each upgrade

## Installation

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser to play!

## Files

- `index.html` - Game structure and UI
- `styles.css` - Visual styling and animations
- `script.js` - Game logic, state management, and event handling

## Browser Support

Works in all modern browsers that support:
